About Carbide Tooling

Precision sourcing for precision machining.

Carbide Tooling connects machine shops, mold makers, and aerospace manufacturers with ISO-certified carbide tooling factories in China. We handle supplier vetting, QC inspection, logistics, and import compliance.

Every order is inspected before shipment. Partner factories hold ISO 9001:2025 certifications with Zeiss CMM and Zoller tool presetting equipment. Batch traceability is documented with full certificates of conformance.

We also maintain a comprehensive library of free CNC calculators and engineering reference tools used by machinists worldwide.
