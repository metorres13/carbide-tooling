About this site.

Why we built it and how it works.

Carbide Tooling is an independent sourcing resource for precision machine shops worldwide. We research, evaluate, and connect buyers with quality Chinese manufacturers of carbide cutting tools.

The problem we solve

Finding reliable tooling suppliers in China is difficult. Trade shows are expensive. Alibaba is hit-or-miss. Language barriers, quality inconsistency, hidden costs — the friction keeps many shops from accessing the competitive pricing Chinese factories can offer.

How we help

We maintain a curated network of vetted manufacturers — each evaluated on product quality, consistency, delivery reliability, and communication. We don't take commissions from factories. Our focus is on helping buyers find the right match for their specific needs.
