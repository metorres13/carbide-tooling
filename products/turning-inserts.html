Grades by Material

  • Steel (P): 通用 grade with AlTiN or CVD coating. CNMG 120408 is the most common size.
  • Stainless (M): Sharp edge grade with AlTiN coating. Better chipbreaker design required.
  • Cast Iron (K): Uncoated or TiCN coated. Higher toughness grades preferred.
  • Aluminum (N): Polished surface, sharp edge, uncoated. Special chipbreaker for aluminum.
  • Hardened (H): Submicron grade with TiSiN or AlCrN coating. For HRC 45+.
